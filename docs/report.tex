\section*{Abstract}
This report contains the Context-Free Grammar (CFG) and the lexical
specification (regular expressions) for a minimal subset of the Julia
programming language used for the Phase 1 deliverable.

\section{Context-Free Grammar (CFG)}
The CFG for the minimal Julia subset (non-terminals are in UPPERCASE for
readability):

\begin{verbatim}
Program         -> StmtList

StmtList        -> Stmt StmtList | \epsilon

Stmt            -> VarDecl | Assignment | IfStmt | WhileStmt | PrintStmt

VarDecl         -> IDENTIFIER "::" Type "=" Expr NEWLINE
                 | IDENTIFIER "=" Expr NEWLINE

Type            -> "Int64" | "Float64" | "Bool" | "String"

Assignment      -> IDENTIFIER "=" Expr NEWLINE

IfStmt          -> "if" Expr NEWLINE StmtList ElseIfList ElseBlock "end" NEWLINE
ElseIfList      -> "elseif" Expr NEWLINE StmtList ElseIfList | \epsilon
ElseBlock       -> "else" NEWLINE StmtList | \epsilon

WhileStmt       -> "while" Expr NEWLINE StmtList "end" NEWLINE

PrintStmt       -> "println" "(" Expr ")" NEWLINE

Expr            -> Expr "||" LogAndExpr | LogAndExpr
LogAndExpr      -> LogAndExpr "&&" NotExpr | NotExpr
NotExpr         -> "!" NotExpr | RelExpr
RelExpr         -> AddExpr RelOp AddExpr | AddExpr
RelOp           -> "==" | "!=" | "<" | ">" | "<=" | ">="
AddExpr         -> AddExpr AddOp MulExpr | MulExpr
AddOp           -> "+" | "-"
MulExpr         -> MulExpr MulOp UnaryExpr | UnaryExpr
MulOp           -> "*" | "/" | "%"
UnaryExpr       -> "-" UnaryExpr | Primary
Primary         -> IDENTIFIER | INT_LIT | FLOAT_LIT | STRING_LIT | BOOL_LIT | "(" Expr ")"

IDENTIFIER      -> [a-zA-Z_][a-zA-Z0-9_]*
INT_LIT         -> [0-9]+
FLOAT_LIT       -> [0-9]+\.[0-9]+
STRING_LIT      -> \"[^\"]*\"
BOOL_LIT        -> "true" | "false"
NEWLINE         -> '\\n' (or ';')
\end{verbatim}

\section{Lexical Specification (Regular Expressions)}
The following table lists the token names and their regular expressions.

\begin{longtable}{p{0.25\textwidth} p{0.7\textwidth}}
Token & Regular expression / literal \\
\hline
IF & "if" \\
ELSEIF & "elseif" \\
ELSE & "else" \\
END & "end" \\
WHILE & "while" \\
PRINTLN & "println" \\
TRUE & "true" \\
FALSE & "false" \\
TYPE_INT & "Int64" \\
TYPE_FLOAT & "Float64" \\
TYPE_BOOL & "Bool" \\
TYPE_STR & "String" \\
IDENTIFIER & [a-zA-Z_][a-zA-Z0-9_]* \\
INT_LIT & [0-9]+ \\
FLOAT_LIT & [0-9]+\.[0-9]+ \\
STRING_LIT & \"[^\"]*\" \\
EQ & ==, NEQ & !=, LT & <, GT & >, LEQ & <=, GEQ & >= \\
AND & &&, OR & ||, NOT & ! \\
ASSIGN & = \\
DCOLON & :: \\
LPAREN / RPAREN & ( / ) \\
SEMICOLON & ; \\
NEWLINE & \\n\end{longtable}

\section{Notes}
- Keywords are matched before identifiers.
- Multi-character operators (==, !=, <=, >=, &&, ||, ::) are matched before
  single-character operators according to the longest-match rule.
